Music Business Management

Elevating Musical Talent

A Symphony of Support

At Frith Nightswan Enterprises, we are passionate about nurturing musical talent and amplifying creative voices in the global music industry. Guided by the exceptional artistry of Frith Hilton, our founder and multifaceted musician, we are laying the groundwork for a future where our music business management services will empower artists to thrive. While our current focus is on Frith’s remarkable contributions in poetry, music, and personal journals, we are excited to share our vision for a comprehensive suite of music business management services.

A Foundation for Musical Success

In the coming years, Frith Nightswan Enterprises plans to offer a range of professional music business management services designed to support artists at every stage of their careers. These include:

      - Artist Management: Providing strategic guidance to help artists navigate the industry, build their brand, and achieve their creative goals.

      - Music Marketing: Crafting tailored marketing campaigns to promote artists’ work, engage audiences, and expand their reach.

      - Royalty Administration: Managing royalty collection and distribution to ensure artists receive fair compensation for their creations.

      - Tour Coordination: Organising seamless tours and live performances, from logistics to audience engagement, to elevate artists’ presence.

These services will embody the same creativity and dedication that define Frith Hilton’s musical journey, ensuring every artist receives unparalleled support to shine in the industry.

Rooted in the Present, Aspiring for the Future

As of 23 August 2025, Frith Nightswan Enterprises relies solely on the extraordinary talents of Frith Hilton, limiting our capacity to offer these music business management services to the public. However, we are diligently working to expand our expertise and resources to make this vision a reality. We are committed to keeping our community informed, and we will share updates through our website and social platforms when these services become available. Our goal is to become a trusted partner for musicians seeking to transform their passion into a sustainable career.
