The Frith Hilton Collection

A Poetic Legacy

The Spark of Inspiration

The Frith Hilton Collection began in 2021 with the evocative phrase, “Cremating of the old tangle,” shared on Twitter (now X). This marked the start of a two-month daily poetry ritual, captivating readers and coinciding with the birthdays of Jessica Andrea (May 1) and Frith Hilton (May 23), infusing the work with personal significance.

Journey to Publication

In 2021, Frith Hilton submitted a manuscript of over ninety poems to Austin Macauley Publishers, hopeful but uncertain about its reception. Although a deal was offered, it fell through, leading him to establish Frith Nightswan Publishers. Under its independent imprint, Forest Crib Books, he released The Forest Collection, a compilation of 117 poems available for free download online.

Connections and Influence

Hilton’s poetry forged a bond with Jessica Andrea, sparked by a birthday tribute. Her friends, including Melanie Fontana and Ryan Potter, praised his lyrical depth, amplifying his reach. This connection enriched his work, particularly within the vibrant community of RATTPACK, fans of Andrea’s former spouse, Logic.

A Dedication to Taylor Swift

The collection gained prominence for its dedication to Taylor Swift, with many poems inspired by her artistry. Hilton hinted at a personal connection, a narrative echoed by Swift’s 2024 album, The Tortured Poets Department. His second poetry collection included a poem titled “Teacher,” acknowledging Swift’s influence on his craft.

Cultural Impact and Recognition

The release of The Forest Collection under the pen name Forest Theo Nightswan drew attention from notable figures, including Kendall and Kylie Jenner, who expressed admiration for Hilton’s poetic voice. His work, steeped in themes from Swift’s Folklore, wove personal narratives with universal emotions, resonating widely.

A Lasting Legacy

The Frith Hilton Collection culminated with its final poetry book on January 2, 2025. Published under Frith Nightswan Publishers, it solidified Hilton’s reputation as a multifaceted artist, blending poetry, music, and storytelling. Today, the collection stands as a testament to his creative journey and enduring influence.
