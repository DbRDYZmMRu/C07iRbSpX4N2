Vision 2020 (A Life Worth Living)

Frith Hilton’s Personal Odyssey

A Journey of Self-Discovery

Vision 2020 (A Life Worth Living) is Frith Hilton’s intimate personal journal, a candid record of the thoughts and aspirations that shaped his path to becoming a writer. Born to Yoruba parents in Sango-Ota, Ogun State, Nigeria, and raised in a Christian household with deep ties to the church, Frith initially thrived as a software developer with a passion for music. In 2019, he released his debut music tape, H.I.V (Heartbreak In View), and a music video for its single, Bleeding Heart. Yet, in 2020, a yearning to explore classic literature—poetry, classical music, and stoicism—ignited a new chapter, setting him on a five-year plan to redefine himself as a writer.

Inspired by Clarity and Ambition

The journal’s title, Vision 2020 (A Life Worth Living), draws inspiration from Justin Timberlake’s 2013 album, The 20/20 Experience, reflecting Frith’s mantra of seeing his future clearly. In late 2018, he quit his software development job, ceased using cannabis, and returned to his parents’ home in Nigeria to prepare for this transformation. A brief visit to Dubai, UAE, in 2019—coincidentally where his future collaborator Mariam Rod resides—renewed his hope and focus. Back in Nigeria, Frith began daily annotations in 2020, capturing ideas and reflections he wished to revisit, laying the groundwork for a life worth living.

A Muse and a Milestone

Frith’s journey was profoundly shaped by meeting Jessica Andrea in 2020, who became his first muse and a guiding force. Their months-long correspondence, parts of which Frith shared publicly in a folder dump on his website, nurtured his writing ambitions and introduced him to the nuances of social media and content presentation. With Mariam Rod’s support, Frith published Vision 2020 unedited in its raw, authentic form, initially dedicating it to her. Republished in 2023 under Forest Crib Books, an imprint of Frith Nightswan Publishers, the journal was rededicated to Taylor Swift, honouring her influence on his creative path.

An Intimate Legacy

Vision 2020 (A Life Worth Living) offers readers a rare glimpse into the mind of Frith Hilton, revealing the personal reflections behind his artistic expressions. Unlike his poetry, this journal is a pleasure read for fans, bridging the gap between the artist and the person. Its unfiltered thoughts resonate with authenticity, inviting readers to connect with Frith’s aspirations and struggles. As a standalone work, it stands as a testament to his transformative journey, cementing his legacy as a multifaceted creative force.
