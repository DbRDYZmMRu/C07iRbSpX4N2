The West to West Collection

Frith Hilton’s Timeless Poetic Journey

A Vision Born in Verse

At Frith Nightswan Publishers, we’re proud to celebrate Frith Hilton’s third poetic masterpiece, The West to West Collection, launched in 2025. This collection draws its heart from a poem Frith penned as a teenager, “WEST to WEST,” first published in Frith Hilton Collection II. Its evocative line, “Tomorrow changes not,” became the guiding theme, echoing a timeless call to “make this love we have remain.” With over five hundred poems across more than twenty books, Frith began sharing daily poetry on his official website, inviting readers to join a journey where love and connection “shower free” through “the windows of heaven’s walls.”

Crafting a Poetic Legacy

The West to West Collection came to life with its first book, published on 31 March 2025 under our cherished imprint, Forest Crib Books. Featuring seven poems dedicated to Donatella Versace, this volume set a bold tone for the series. Contrary to some readers’ thoughts, the title isn’t a nod to the Re-Animator film’s Mr. West but a reflection of Frith’s own “WEST to WEST,” a poem that urges us to “seek perfection for the helpless path.” As the collection grows, it continues to evolve through ongoing publications, each weaving the theme of enduring love and care “from all around in our world.” Frith’s daily website posts keep readers immersed, making this a living, breathing work of art.

Honouring Icons, Connecting Hearts

The West to West Collection shines with dedications to luminaries who inspire Frith, including Kylie Jenner, Madonna, Taylor Swift, Princess Leonor of Asturias, Tate McRae, and Charli XCX. Each dedication embodies the collection’s core belief that “this love we have” unites us all, as expressed in the original poem’s heartfelt plea. These tributes, paired with Frith’s lyrical depth, have forged connections with readers and amplified the collection’s reach, resonating with the same passion that drives us at Frith Nightswan Publishers to share his work.

A Resonance That Endures

Building on Frith’s legacy as an innovative poet, The West to West Collection is a celebration of connection and creativity. Its universal themes, rooted in the promise to “show how much we care,” have touched hearts worldwide, from devoted fans to cultural icons. Published under Forest Crib Books, this ongoing series continues to grow, with each new volume adding to Frith’s remarkable journey. As we share his poetry through our website and publications, we’re excited to see this collection inspire and unite, proving that tomorrow, indeed, changes not.
