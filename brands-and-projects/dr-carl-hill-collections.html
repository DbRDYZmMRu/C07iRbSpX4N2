The Dr. Carl Hill Collection

A Poetic Tribute

A Cinematic Spark

Inspired by the cult-classic Re-Animator film series, Frith Hilton’s Dr. Carl Hill Collection began with its first poetry book, released on January 20, 2025. Drawing from his fascination with the character Dr. Carl Hill, Hilton named the collection after him, weaving its poems around the evocative theme “Across the Bridge.” Dedicated to President Joe Biden, this debut set the stage for a series that blends cinematic inspiration with lyrical depth.

A Growing Legacy

The Dr. Carl Hill Collection comprises five poetry books, each dedicated to a notable figure: President Joe Biden, Caitlyn Jenner, Lisa of BLACKPINK, Victoria Justice, and Kathleen Elizabeth Hilton. The final book, honoring Kathleen Elizabeth Hilton, was released on March 10, 2025. With its rich thematic exploration, the series has captivated readers, and future installments are anticipated.

Creative Evolution

Hilton’s creative process was ignited by the first film, Re-Animator of the Re-Animator films. After penning five books, his team recommended watching the sequel, Bride of Re-Animator, to inspire further additions to the collection. Its cinematic influence to shape Hilton’s poetic voice, merging pop culture with introspective verse.
